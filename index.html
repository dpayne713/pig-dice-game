-2 players, playing in rounds - In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as they want. Each result get added to their ROUND score. if the player rolls a 1, all his ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn
- The player can 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn
- The first player to reach 100 points on GLOBAL score wins the game
